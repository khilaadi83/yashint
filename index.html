Centre Tyre Trailed Offset Disc Harrow

“Center Offset Disc Harrow” has a strong and sturdy main frame with high quality steel discs and heavy duty chilled cast iron spools to provide centre weight. Optional wheels can be provided for towing on road form inner side oil seal and outer side threaded outer cap to prevent dust. Due to steering, tyre attachment easy to use & transportation.

# Features
1 Heavy duty cast iron spools to provide centre weight.
2 Optional wheels can be provided for easy transportation.
3 It has a strong and sturdy main frame with Boron steel discs.
