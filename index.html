About

Yash Internationals PVT LTD is an integrated logistics provider specializing in all phases of transportation and supply chain management services.We provide freight forwarding, warehousing & distribution and logistics services. With years of experience, we bring an unrivalled reputation for reliability and competence to the logistics & shipping industry. Our partnerships, membership and agencies with various accredited international networks enable us to provide our services globally. Our team has a vast amount of experience in various logistical areas. We provide flexible and customized solutions to simplify our client’s freight requirement.
